Who we are

TheAquascape are a group of people dedicated to providing the best information possible on all things planted aquarium. Whether it's your first tank or your tenth, we have all the information you need to keep your tank healthy and running.

What we offer

Our database includes everything from water care, proper tank cycing, algae control, flora type, and a long list of Fauna that is suitable for all tank sizes.
